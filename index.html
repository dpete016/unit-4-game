CRYSTALS COLLECTOR

MAGIC NUMBER:

You have four crystals and a random number. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. Clicking on the crystals adds up a specific number of points towards the total score. Value of each crystal are a mystery to solve and going over the total point results in a loss. The value of the crystal also changes each reset, so watch out for that and good luck!
